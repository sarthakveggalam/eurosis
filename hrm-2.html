Consistent and Target Salary Tables

Consistent and Target Salary Tables

REECONTRO is a local organization in South of Mozambique and contributes to the development and support of Orphan and Vulnerable Children (OVCs). The Organization benefitted from interventions under the LCD.

This story (problem, intervention and results) is about the development of simple and effective worksheets for salary processing at REECONTRO, considering that the Organization has a staff of 30 people.

The problem

REECONTRO used to process salaries in not so appropriate Excel Worksheets, which would take 2 working days while this could be done in few minutes. The worksheets were not properly protected and there was no link with salary receipts.

Thus the data recorded on the salary worksheet was later recorded again to produce salary receipts. This involved the risk of mistakes, information being easily changed, and difficulty in tracking changes.

A simple addition or elimination of a digit could be costly and hard to notice.

The salary sheets and respective receipts were not properly electronic filed. Monthly, older files were being replaced by new ones, thus eliminating electronic support of past processing.

The intervention

Through the LCD initiative, EUROSIS’ technical assistance team and REENCONTRO’s staff developed a more adequate worksheet for processing salaries, linked to the salary receipts of all staff.

EUROSIS’ technical assistance team worked with REENCONTRO to: a) identify the main constraints of the system in place;

b) define requirements for a proper salary and salary receipts processing; c) design and develop a worksheet according to the requirements; d) test and implement the new worksheet; e) provide on-the-job training to the Admin and Financial Management Staff; and f) provide remote support to ensure sustainable use of the new platform.

The results

REENCONTRO’s salary processing is now faster and more accurate. FM staff is now equipped with adequate worksheets for processing salaries and salary receipts. Now FM staff take only a few minutes to process salaries, instead of 2 days prior to the intervention.

The salary receipts are now linked to the salary sheet.

The risk of mistakes, information being easily changed, and difficulty in tracking changes is now significantly less than it was before the intervention.

The electronic files are now properly filed in dropbox folders. These files are easily accessible to relevant and authorized staff for faster salary processing, approval and payment.
